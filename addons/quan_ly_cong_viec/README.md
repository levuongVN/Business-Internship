# Quan Ly Cong Viec - De tai 1

## Muc tieu
- Quan ly khach hang va nhan vien phu trach.
- Ghi nhan tuong tac cham soc khach hang.
- Sinh nhieu cong viec tu mot tuong tac.
- Theo doi qua trinh xu ly va ket qua cong viec.
- Tao hop dong nhe khi qua trinh cham soc dat muc ky ket.
- Ho tro AI cho goi y uu tien va goi y dau viec.

## Module lien quan
- `nhan_su`
- `quan_ly_khach_hang`
- `quan_ly_cong_viec`

## Luong nghiep vu
1. Tao khach hang va gan nhan vien phu trach.
2. Ghi nhan tuong tac: goi dien, bao gia, lich hen, cham soc khac.
3. Sinh cong viec thu cong hoac bang AI tu tuong tac.
4. Theo doi cong viec theo deadline, nguon tao, muc uu tien, nhan vien.
5. Tao hop dong nhe khi khach dong y chot.
6. Theo doi hop dong theo trang thai va ngay het han.

## Tinh nang chinh
- Smart button tren khach hang va nhan vien.
- Tree/Form/Search/Kanban/Pivot/Graph cho cong viec.
- Tree/Form/Search cho tuong tac va hop dong.
- AI wizard de xem truoc va chon cac dau viec se tao.
- Bao cao cong viec theo nhan vien va trang thai.
- Danh sach khach hang can cham soc, hop dong sap het han.

## Cai dat
1. Cap nhat module `quan_ly_khach_hang`.
2. Cap nhat module `quan_ly_cong_viec`.
3. Neu dung AI, cau hinh `gemini_api_key` trong `odoo.conf`.
4. Nap demo data neu muon co san kich ban thuyet trinh.

## Demo nhanh
1. Mo menu `Quan ly khach hang` de xem khach hang.
2. Mo menu `Quan ly cong viec / Tuong tac khach hang` de tao tuong tac.
3. Tu form tuong tac, bam `AI goi y dau viec` hoac `Sinh cong viec`.
4. Theo doi cong viec trong Kanban/Pivot/Graph.
5. Tao hop dong tu khach hang hoac cong viec da hoan thanh.
