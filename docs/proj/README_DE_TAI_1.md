# De tai 1 - Quan ly khach hang + Quan ly cong viec

## Bai toan
He thong quan ly mot khach hang tu luc tiep nhan thong tin, ghi nhan tuong tac, sinh cac dau viec follow-up, giao viec cho nhan vien, theo doi tien do, den giai doan tao hop dong nhe khi khach chap nhan.

## 4 thuc the chinh
- Khach hang
- Tuong tac khach hang
- Cong viec
- Hop dong khach hang

## Diem noi bat
- Mot tuong tac co the sinh nhieu cong viec.
- Cong viec gan duoc voi khach hang, nhan vien va tuong tac nguon.
- AI goi y muc do uu tien va danh sach dau viec de tao nhanh.
- Hop dong duoc quan ly o muc nhe, phuc vu bai toan chot khach.
