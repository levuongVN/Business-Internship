# Kich ban demo

1. Tao hoac mo mot khach hang da co san.
2. Tu khach hang, tao mot tuong tac moi dang la `bao_gia` hoac `lich_hen`.
3. Nhap noi dung nhu cau cua khach hang.
4. Bam `AI goi y dau viec` de mo wizard goi y.
5. Chon cac dau viec can tao va nhan tao cong viec.
6. Vao menu `Cong viec`, chuyen trang thai, cap nhat ket qua xu ly.
7. Tu cong viec da hoan thanh hoac tu khach hang, tao hop dong nhe.
8. Vao menu `Bao cao` de xem khach hang can cham soc va hop dong sap het han.
